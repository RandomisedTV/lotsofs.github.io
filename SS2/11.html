Potential scoring opportunities

  • The 4 aircraft flying by at the start of the level yield 500 points each, if a way can be found to destroy them with the given tools.
  • 3 Dropships appear in the level, each worth 100 points. They have 999999 health and some are marked as invulnerable.
  • The neanderthal in the teleporter is worth 100 points, but is marked as invulnerable until destroyed by the scripted sequence.
  • None of these are considered enemies and thus none of them would add to the kill tally.
